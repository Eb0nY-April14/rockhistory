Welcome

This is christYmet History Website, a website created to provide useful information to users, lovers of history, tourists and potential investors about the famous olumo Rock and the history that surrounds it which is an heritage of the Egba people in the south west region of Nigeria. On a larger scale, it aims at preserving the cultural heritage of the Yoruba people for posterity so that it does not go into extinction.

The focus here is about a rock located in Abeokuta, a state in the south west region of Nigeria called Olumo Rock. We will provide you with the history surrounding this rock, facts and figures, timeline of events and activities that revolves around this priceless monument, which is one of the gifts of God to mankind.
